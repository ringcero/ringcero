Privacy Specification

Version 1.0 // Last Revised: 28 Aug 2025

1.0 Data Collection

We collect information you voluntarily provide. The only personally identifiable information we actively collect is your email address when you subscribe to our report notifications. Our forms are protected by Cloudflare Turnstile, which may process technical data for spam mitigation as per their own policies.

2.0 Data Utilization

Your email address is used exclusively for operational purposes: to deliver notifications when new reports are released, and to respond directly to inquiries you send to our contact address. Your data will not be sold, leased, or distributed.

3.0 Security & Third-Party Protocols

We employ secure, reputable third-party services for form submissions. While we prioritize data integrity, no method of digital transmission is infallible. We accept no liability for third-party data breaches.

4.0 User Autonomy & Opt-Out

You retain full control over your data. Every notification email we dispatch contains a functional "unsubscribe" link. Activating this link will permanently purge your email address from our active mailing list.

5.0 Inquiries

For questions or clarifications regarding this specification, contact our primary channel: contact@ringcero.com.
